Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Ritalu (“we,” “us”) collects and uses your information when you visit www.ritalu.xyz and join our waitlist. Ritalu is a pre-launch wellness product, and this policy covers our website and waitlist only.

Information we collect

  • Information you give us. When you join the waitlist, we collect your name and email address, and any answers you choose to share in our optional survey.
  • Information collected automatically. We use analytics and advertising tools to understand how people find and use our site. These may collect standard technical data such as your device and browser type, approximate location (from your IP address), pages visited, and how you arrived (for example, from an ad). We may use cookies or similar technologies for this.

How we use your information

  • To manage the waitlist and let you know when Ritalu launches.
  • To understand which channels bring people to Ritalu and to improve our website and product.
  • To respond to you if you contact us.

Who we share it with

We use trusted third-party services to run our website and waitlist, and they process data on our behalf:

  • Google (Forms, Sheets, Analytics) — to collect and store waitlist responses and measure site usage.
  • Meta (Instagram) and TikTok — advertising tools to measure and improve our ads, when enabled.

We do not sell your personal information, and we don’t share it for anyone else’s independent marketing.

Cookies & tracking

Our site may use cookies and similar technologies for analytics and advertising. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Your choices and rights

  • You can unsubscribe or ask us to delete your information at any time by emailing info@ritalu.xyz.
  • Depending on where you live (for example, the EU/UK or California), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your data, and to object to certain uses. To exercise these, email info@ritalu.xyz.

Data retention

We keep your waitlist information until Ritalu launches or you ask us to delete it, whichever comes first.

International transfers

Because we use services like Google, Meta, and TikTok, your information may be processed in countries other than your own, including the United States.

Children

Ritalu is not intended for anyone under 16, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information, though no method of transmission or storage over the internet is 100% secure.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we’ll change the “last updated” date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at info@ritalu.xyz.

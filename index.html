FAQ

Questions, answered

Everything you need to know about Ritalu. Still curious? Reach out to the team.

Ritalu is an operating system for women’s well-being — one private platform that brings your cycle, sleep, nutrition, biomarkers, fitness, and daily habits together and turns them into simple, personal guidance, so you can understand your body and act with context.

Women who want to understand their body over time — especially anyone tired of juggling separate apps for cycle, sleep, nutrition, and labs, and who wants one clear picture instead of scattered data.

Each of those does one thing — a period app logs your cycle, a wearable measures sleep or recovery. Ritalu connects them: it brings your cycle, wearables, labs, and daily habits into one place and turns them into a single daily read on what to focus on — something no single app or device does on its own.

Each day, Ritalu reads your cycle phase and recent signals — sleep, energy, and more — and gives you a short, personal brief on what to prioritize: how to move, eat, and recover that day. The more you use it, the more tailored it gets.

No. Ritalu is a wellness and self-knowledge tool, not a medical device — it doesn’t diagnose, treat, or replace your doctor. It helps you understand your own patterns and make everyday decisions with more context.

Your data is encrypted and always under your control. We never sell it. You decide what to connect, and you can export or delete everything at any time.
